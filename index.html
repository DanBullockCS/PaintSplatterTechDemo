Paint Splatter Tech Demo

    General Controls
  • Click on the window to start game
  • Esc - Get Out of Game Window
  • WASD - Movement
  • Left Click / Space - Shoot Paint
  • Left Shift - Sprint
  • Left Alt - Fast Sprint
  • Left Shift + Left Alt - LightSpeed Sprint

    Hints
  • There are 8 enemies to find on the map, try to find and shoot them all!
  • Try lightspeed jumping off of the terrain, it's quite fun to fly around with it
